How to use this tool

This tool can be used to generate cases studies for the 2013 Economic Impact Repot. In order to generate PDFs:

1. Update content in the spreadsheet
2. Update state images in Google Drive
3. Update case study images in Google Drive
4. Click on "Generate PDFs" to the right.
